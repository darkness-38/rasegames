info About pH

Acidic (pH 0-6)

High H⁺ concentration. Sour taste, corrosive.

Neutral (pH 7)

Equal H⁺ and OH⁻. Pure water.

Basic (pH 8-14)

High OH⁻ concentration. Bitter, slippery.

pH Formula

pH = -log[H⁺]

pH is the negative logarithm of hydrogen ion concentration
