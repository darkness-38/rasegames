lightbulb About Diffusion

Graham's Law: The rate of diffusion is inversely proportional to the square root of the molar mass.

r₁/r₂ = √(M₂/M₁)

Key Factors:

  • Higher temperature → faster diffusion
  • Lower mass → faster diffusion
  • Smaller radius → faster diffusion
  • Higher concentration gradient → faster diffusion
